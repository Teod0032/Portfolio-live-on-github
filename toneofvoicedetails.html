Tone of Voice

Client: Punk Rock Kitty Boots (Example)

This project focused on defining a unique and impactful tone of voice that resonates with the target audience, ensuring consistent and engaging brand communication across all platforms. We aimed for a voice that is both rebellious and playful, capturing the essence of the brand's edgy yet fun-loving spirit.

The Challenge & Approach

The primary challenge was to differentiate the "Punk Rock Kitty Boots" brand in a saturated youth fashion market. Our approach involved deep-dive persona development, competitor analysis, and collaborative workshops with the client to unearth the core brand personality.

We then crafted comprehensive tone of voice guidelines, including core messaging pillars, a unique vocabulary lexicon, examples of on-brand copy for various channels (social media, product descriptions, ad copy), and clear do's and don'ts for humor and engagement.

Skills & Tools Utilized

  • Concept & Branding Strategy
  • Creative Marketing
  • Product Photography & Redesign
  • Persona Development
  • Figma
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Adobe Firefly AI

Project Outcome

The newly defined tone of voice was successfully implemented across all brand communications, leading to a reported 30% increase in social media engagement and a more cohesive, recognizable brand identity that resonated strongly with the target demographic.
