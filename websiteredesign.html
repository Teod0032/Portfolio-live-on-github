Website Re-design

Client: Blommenslyst Camping (Example)

This project involved a complete overhaul of an existing website to improve user experience, modernize the design, and increase online bookings. The focus was on creating an intuitive, visually appealing, and mobile-responsive platform that truly reflected the client's brand.

The Challenge & Approach

The client's previous website was outdated, suffered from poor navigation, and was not optimized for mobile devices, leading to a high bounce rate and missed conversion opportunities. The primary goal was to create a welcoming, user-friendly, and informative site that made it effortless for visitors to find necessary information and complete bookings.

Our approach began with thorough user research and persona development to understand the target audience's needs and pain points. This was followed by wireframing and creating iterative design mockups in Figma. We emphasized clear calls-to-action, high-quality imagery showcasing the location, and a streamlined booking process. Principles of UI/UX psychology were applied throughout to guide user flow and enhance overall satisfaction. User testing at various stages helped validate design decisions.

Skills & Tools Utilized

  • Persona Development
  • UI/UX Psychology
  • User Testing
  • Figma
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Adobe Firefly AI

Project Outcome

The redesigned website resulted in a 40% increase in online bookings within the first three months of launch. User feedback highlighted a significantly improved navigation experience and a more professional, trustworthy brand image. The bounce rate decreased by 60%.
